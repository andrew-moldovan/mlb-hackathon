Types of Pitches

The chart below shows the distribution of a pitcher's pitch types, their effectiveness and usage.

The green color represents the % of positive pitches over all pitches for that pitch type. To determine what a positive/negative/neutral pitch was we look at the pitchResult and in the case where a pitchResult is an IP (In Play) then we check the paResult. The code that does this analysis and the classification of pitch results can be found in pitch-type.js.

Average hits per inning

The bar chart below shows the average number of hits that a pitcher has against him per inning. It's an average because we divide the total number of hits in a single inning over the number of times the pitcher pitched in that inning.

It's interesting to see whether some pitchers give up all their hits at the end of the game, or at the beginning or if it's more evenly spread out.
